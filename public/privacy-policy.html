Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Our Privacy Mission

At the heart of our mission is a simple belief: your data belongs to you. We design all of our applications with a "Privacy First" approach, ensuring that your digital experience remains private and secure.

2. Core Principles

We offer various types of applications. Depending on the specific app you are using, the following principles apply:

A. Ad-Supported Applications (Free)

Some of our applications use third-party advertising networks, such as Google AdMob, to provide content at no cost to you. These advertising networks may collect certain device-specific information (such as IDFA/AAID) to serve relevant advertisements. You have control over ad tracking preferences through your device's privacy settings.

B. Premium Applications (Ad-Free)

Our premium applications, or applications where you have purchased an ad removal subscription, are built without any third-party ads or tracking SDKs for marketing purposes. These versions are designed to collect zero personal data for advertising.

C. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

All financial transactions are handled securely via Apple StoreKit. We never see, process, or store your payment details or billing address on our servers.

3. Localized Data Processing

We believe in decentralized data. Features that require device sensors or permissions (such as location access) are processed locally on your device. No location data is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.

4. Voice Data and Artificial Intelligence

For applications utilizing voice recording, dictation, or Artificial Intelligence (AI) features, we transparently handle your data as follows to strictly protect your privacy:

  • Voice Recordings & Transcription (Apple Speech): When you record audio for transcription purposes, the app requires microphone access. Your voice recordings are sent securely to Apple's Speech Recognition service to accurately transcribe the audio into text. We do not store these audio files on external servers; they remain on your local device.
  • Text Processing (Google Gemini AI): To provide intelligent features such as note summarization, translation, and action-item extraction, the text contents of your notes may be securely transmitted to Google's Gemini API.
  • User Consent & Optionality: You have the explicit choice to opt-in or opt-out of these AI features at any time. If you decline AI features, your text notes will not be sent to Google, and the app will operate locally. We do not use your voice or text data to train our own AI models, and data shared with these third-party services is subject to their respective robust privacy and security protections.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our systems. Since we don't collect or store your personal identity data on our servers, your digital footprint remains minimal and secure.
