Technical Support

Q: The app is crashing. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  • Close and restart the app
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us with details about when the crash occurs
Q: The app won't load data. Why?
A: Make sure you have an active internet connection. Our app requires internet to fetch data. Check your Wi-Fi or cellular connection and try again.
Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Some features may work offline if data has been cached. However, most features require an internet connection to function properly.
