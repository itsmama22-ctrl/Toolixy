Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Your feedback drives our innovation. Please email us with a description of your idea or the issue you encountered. Screenshots or videos are always helpful!
How do I manage my premium subscription?
All subscriptions are managed securely by Apple. You can view, change, or cancel them at any time via your iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions.
Why do some apps show advertisements?
Ads allow us to offer free versions of our tools. For a pure, "Elite" experience, many of our apps offer ad-free premium versions via In-App Purchase.
How is my location data used?
For spiritual utilities (like Nur), location is used only to calculate local prayer times. This processing happens 100% locally on your device and is never shared with our servers.
